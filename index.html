Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need To Know

Common questions about SCANME and vehicle QR protection.

Does the QR show my personal information?

No. SCANME never displays the owner's name, address or any personal details.

What happens after someone scans my QR?

They only see secure communication buttons like Call, WhatsApp, SMS, Email and Emergency Contact.

Can I change my phone number later?

Yes. You can update your contact information anytime from your dashboard.

Can I report my vehicle as stolen?

Yes. Change the vehicle status to Lost or Stolen and everyone scanning the QR will immediately see the updated status.

Does SCANME support commercial vehicles?

Yes. Cars, bikes, scooters, taxis, buses, trucks and complete fleets are supported.
